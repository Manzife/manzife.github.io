About

I am a Data Scientist and Economist focused on pricing, experimentation, and quantitative decision-making. My work connects economic theory, statistics, and product analytics to solve real business problems.

I hold a degree in Economics from the University of São Paulo (USP) and work at the intersection of data science, experimentation, and strategy, with experience in credit analytics and pricing optimization.

My main interests include causal inference, marketplace dynamics, experimentation frameworks, and applied machine learning for business decisions.
